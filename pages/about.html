About Us

Rusty Browns started out small. Like real small, not big or huge or anything like that. Just a small little start, kind of tiny really, in the back of this dry cleaner place where clothes got cleaned. The kitchen area was not very clean, but it was there and was a space where donut stuff could happen and be made.

Then, after some time passed and moved on, things started changing slowly but also a bit quickly. One shop became two, and then more came after that, so the number of shops got bigger over time. We made a system so that more and more people in more places could get doughnuts from us.

The idea, or maybe more like the plan or goal we had in mind, was always the same: to make people smile and feel good and happy from eating sweet stuff. Doughnuts have sugar and they're fried and hot and people eat them and feel good. It's just what it is and that's how it goes.

Doughnuts from Rusty Brown's isn't just food you eat, it's more than just something to chew. It is a food item that is also kind of a moment or a feeling or even maybe a little memory that stays. The outside is a bit crunchy but not hard-crunchy and the inside is soft in a way that's not hard but more like squishy-soft. Some people like them, some people like them a lot, and others like them less but still maybe kind of like them.
